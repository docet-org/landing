What Docet does

Every core tool, with AI built in from the start.

✦ AI — Session notes

Voice memo → polished notes in seconds

After a session, a tutor records a quick voice memo. Docet transcribes it, structures it into a proper session note, and queues it for review before it reaches parents. No typing. Notes actually get written.

✦ AI — Retention

Know who's about to leave before they cancel

Docet monitors attendance patterns, engagement signals, and communication gaps — surfacing at-risk students weeks before they'd normally come up. You get the flag early enough to act on it.

Parent updates that actually get sent

AI drafts personalized weekly digests for each student. Directors review and approve before anything goes out. Parents stay informed without your team writing updates individually.

One dashboard across all your locations

Scheduling, billing, CRM, and session history — unified. Franchise owners see enrollment, attendance, and retention across every location without waiting on individual reports.
